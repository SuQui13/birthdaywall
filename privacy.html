Birthday Wishes Wall

Privacy, plainly

Last updated: July 2026

What this is

A Birthday Wishes Wall is a private web page for one celebration, where invited guests leave wishes for the birthday person. This page explains what information the wall stores and what happens to it.

What we store

  • Wishes — the name a guest types, their message, the category they pick, and (optionally) a photo they upload.
  • Gift participation — whether a guest marked "I chipped in", and optionally an amount.
  • Wall settings — the birthday person's first name, the chosen theme, and the host's payment link and gift goal.

That's the full list. There are no accounts, no passwords for guests, no tracking pixels, no analytics, and no cookies. The wall doesn't collect email addresses or phone numbers.

Gift amounts stay secret

If a guest enters a contribution amount, it is used for one thing only: moving the progress bar. It is never displayed to anyone — not to other guests, not to the birthday person — and the system is built so that amounts physically never leave the database.

Payments themselves happen entirely on the payment service the host chose (for example PayPal). The wall never sees, holds, or processes money.

Who can see the wall

Each wall has its own unguessable link. Anyone who has the guest link can read the wishes and add their own — so treat the link like a party invitation and share it only with the guest list. Walls are not listed anywhere or indexed by search engines through us.

How long we keep it

A wall accepts new wishes until shortly after the event date, then becomes read-only. The stored wishes and photos are kept so the birthday person can revisit them and print their keepsake, and are deleted on request at any time (see below). Photos are resized on your device before upload — full-size originals never leave your phone.

Where it's stored

Wall data is stored with our database provider, Supabase, in the region chosen when the service was set up.

Your rights

Hosts can delete any individual wish from their wall at any time using their host link. To have a photo, a wish, or an entire wall permanently deleted — or to ask anything about your data — write to [YOUR-SUPPORT-EMAIL] and it will be handled within a few days.

The demo wall

The try-it demo stores everything only in your own browser on your own device. Nothing you type in the demo is sent anywhere.
