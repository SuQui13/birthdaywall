Questions, answered

Do my guests need to install anything?

No — the wall is a web page. Guests open the link (or scan the QR code) on any phone, tablet or computer and pin their wish right there.

How does the money for the group gift work?

You add your own payment link (PayPal.me, Bizum, Revolut…) and guests pay each other's way directly through it. The wall never holds or processes money — it just shows a percentage-only progress bar and marks contributors' wishes with a 🎁.

Can anyone see how much someone gave?

No. Amounts are optional, and if entered they're used only to move the progress bar. They are never displayed anywhere — not to guests, not to the birthday person.

How long does the wall stay up?

The wall accepts wishes for 60 days (or until shortly after your event date). After that it becomes a read-only keepsake — and you can print it as a book or save it as a PDF anytime.

Is the wall private?

Each wall has its own unguessable link that only the people you share it with know. Anyone with the guest link can read and add wishes — so share it with the guest list, not the whole internet.
